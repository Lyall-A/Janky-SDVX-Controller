## Flashing the firmware
* Hold down the BOOTSEL button while plugging it in
* Drag `Janky-SDVX-Controller.uf2` into the detected drive called RPI-RP2

## GPIO
* `GP2`: BT-A
* `GP3`: BT-B
* `GP4`: BT-C
* `GP5`: BT-D
* `GP8`: FX-L
* `GP9`: FX-R
* `GP6`: START
* `GP14` & `GP15`: VOL-L
* `GP16` & `GP17`: VOL-R
